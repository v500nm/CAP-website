About Us!

Citizens for Animal Protection (C.A.P) Foundation is a registered not-for-profit organization (MAH/319/2020) based in Thane, Mumbai, Maharashtra,India with an aim to work towards expanding the circle of compassion towards animals. We run Thane city’s first animal shelter cum educational center which gives shelter to all kinds of animals and promotes peaceful coexistence among all kinds of species including humans.

Freedom Farm

Freedom Farm is a humble abode being built by Citizens for Animal Protection Foundation for all kinds of animals. It is located on the outskirts of Thane City, near Waghbil Gaon. Our goal is to create a self-sustaining shelter where we plan to create a structure which could help us generate all the basic resources within. We are working towards creating a sustainable structure in collaboration with an organization called ‘MEE’ from the environment perceptive.

Ambulance

We are happy to announce our first Animal Ambulance in Thane. We at Citizens For Animal Protection (CAP) Foundation would like to express our deepest gratitude to team PAWS & Nilesh Bhanage for gifting us their old ambulance. We receive on average 20-30 distress calls throughout the day, it'll surely help us in increasing our response time and reducing the mortality rate of animals in distress. The ambulance service will be functional 11 hours a day for 6 days a week with an experienced driver and trained member of our rescue team. We'll be charging a nominal fee to keep it functional and support our shelter Freedom Farm

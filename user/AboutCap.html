About us

Citizens for Animal Protection (C.A.P) Foundation is a registered not-for-profit organization (MAH/319/2020) based in Thane, Mumbai, Maharashtra,India with an aim to work towards expanding the circle of compassion towards animals. We run Thane city’s first animal shelter cum educational center which gives shelter to all kinds of animals and promotes peaceful coexistence among all kinds of species including humans.

● Freedom Farm is a humble abode being built by Citizens for Animal Protection Foundation for all kinds of animals. ● It is located on the outskirts of Thane City, near Waghbil Gaon. ● Our goal is to create a self-sustaining shelter where we plan to create a structure which could help us generate all the basic resources within.

1. Beliefs :
We believe everyone has a right to live in harmony, peace, and to be treated with compassion. We are trying to change the attitude towards animals by making people empathetic, tolerant, and pacifist. In our view animals are creators entitled to live of freedom with whom we can live side-by-side harmoniously without hurting, abusing or exploiting them and without taking their flesh, their liberty, and their soul.
2. Mission:
Our mission is to empower people with the right approach to help animals in need and create a chain effect by educating and inspiring them on a daily basis through our work.
3. Vision :
Our aim is to work towards expanding the circle of compassion towards animals. We believe that everyone has a right to live in harmony, peace and be treated with compassion.

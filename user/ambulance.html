ANIMAL AMBULANCE IN THANE!


Ambulance

We are happy to announce our first Animal Ambulance in Thane. We at Citizens For Animal Protection (CAP) Foundation would like to express our deepest gratitude to team PAWS & Nilesh Bhanage for gifting us their old ambulance. We receive on average 20-30 distress calls throughout the day, it'll surely help us in increasing our response time and reducing the mortality rate of animals in distress. The ambulance service will be functional 11 hours a day for 6 days a week with an experienced driver and trained member of our rescue team. We'll be charging a nominal fee to keep it functional and support our shelter Freedom Farm

Here are the details for all the animal lovers, Animal shelters, Foster homes, NGO's, vets and pet parents regarding the animal ambulance in Thane District.

We aim to provide quality and affordable services to all the animals in need.

Our services include:
  1. Critical/ICU ambulance
  2. Emergency Transport
  3. Non-emergency
  4. Pet/veterinary ambulance
  5. Special event medical standby services
  6. Burial and Cremation

The ambulance service will be available by appointment only, except for emergency cases even at night subject to the availability of the driver and staff.

Base Fare: 100 rs - 1km Km price - 25rs p/km after 1 km Waiting change - 150rs - 30 min.

The ambulance will be disinfected and sanitized after every trip as a standard procedure. The appointment can be taken by calling and sharing the necessary details on Ambulance Helpline at +91 90762 12169 For any feedback/suggestions or observations, you can write to us on capambulanceservice@gmail.com PS: all the payments will go towards keeping it functional and supporting the critical care centre at our shelter

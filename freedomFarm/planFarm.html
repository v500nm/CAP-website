Through the Freedom Farm, we aim to reach and help out to as many animals as possible once the complete structure is built. There would be an in-house vet who would be attending to all our animals at the Shelter. We plan to create a structure that could potentially house about 100-150 animals at a time. We wish to create a structure that not only helps us take care of animals but is also sustainable to the environment. In terms of the Educational aspect, we would be conducting interactive sessions, workshops and field trips for different schools and colleges. Our ultimate aim is to raise awareness and spread the compassion towards animals.
